# Daily Workflow

Use `/dashboard/daily` before considering any option sale.

Review order:

1. Market regime.
2. Account status.
3. Cash reserve.
4. Open risk warnings.
5. Top CSP opportunities.
6. Top covered call opportunities.
7. Stocks near support.
8. Earnings risk list.
9. Trades requiring action.
10. Journal review.

Do not open a trade unless thesis, assignment plan, exit plan, and maximum-loss scenario are documented.
